This Privacy Policy explains how IVF Help collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit www.ivfhelp.org. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the site.

Section 01

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name and email address (if you submit a contact form or request a quote)
  • City and treatment preference (if you use the free quote tool)
  • Comments or messages you send us

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our site, the following is collected automatically by us and third-party services (including Google AdSense):

  • Device and browser data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage data: pages visited, time on page, referring URLs, links clicked
  • Approximate location: derived from your IP address
  • Cookies and tracking identifiers: described in Section 4
Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense and partners
  • Send requested cost estimates and clinic information
  • Analyse usage trends and understand visitor behaviour
  • Respond to enquiries and provide support
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations
  • Detect and prevent fraud or security threats
Section 03

Google AdSense & Advertising

Required AdSense disclosure: Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. As part of this service:

  • Google and its partners use cookies and similar technologies to collect data about your visits to show relevant ads.
  • Google may use the DoubleClick cookie and other identifiers to serve interest-based (personalised) ads.
  • Google may share information with advertising partners per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not control Google's data collection. Their practices are governed by their own policies.

Opt out of personalised advertising

  • Visit Google Ads Settings to manage your preferences.
  • Visit aboutads.info to opt out of interest-based advertising from participating networks.
  • If in the EEA, visit youronlinechoices.eu.
  • Install the Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

Section 06

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Advertising partners: Data shared with Google and partners for ad delivery (Section 3).
  • Service providers: Hosting, analytics vendors under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: Where required by law or court order.

California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have specific rights under CCPA/CPRA: the right to know what data is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of data sale. We do not sell personal information. Contact us to exercise your rights.

European residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under the GDPR. Our legal bases for processing include legitimate interests, legal obligations, and consent. To exercise your rights, contact us using the details in Section 13.

Section 08

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or to meet legal requirements. When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

Google AdSense and Analytics retain data per their own retention schedules, which we do not directly control.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Section 10

Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. You provide information at your own risk.

Section 11

International Users

IVF Help is operated from India. If you access the site from outside India — including the EU, UK, or the US — your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws.

By using our website, you consent to such transfer. Where required by law, we will put appropriate safeguards in place.
