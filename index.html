What Makes Up the Total Cost?

Itemised breakdown for a standard IVF cycle in India (2026 estimates)

Typically included in base price

  • Initial consultation with fertility specialist
  • Baseline investigations (ultrasound + blood panel)
  • Egg retrieval (OPU) procedure
  • Embryo culture (3–5 days in lab)
  • Embryo transfer procedure
  • Luteal phase support (2 weeks post-ET)
  • One follow-up consultation

Usually billed separately (ask upfront!)

  • Ovarian stimulation injections (₹35,000–₹1,00,000)
  • ICSI micromanipulation (+₹15,000–₹30,000)
  • Embryo freezing for unused embryos
  • Pre-implantation genetic testing (PGT)
  • Anaesthesia fee (sometimes billed separately)
  • Donor eggs or donor sperm (if needed)
  • Repeat monitoring scans beyond package
Pro tip: When comparing clinics, always ask: "Can you give me the total all-inclusive estimate including medications, ICSI if needed, and monitoring scans?" The base price is rarely what you actually pay.
