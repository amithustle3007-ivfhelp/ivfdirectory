Guide to Mumbai's Top IVF Clinics

Nova IVF Fertility — Andheri West

Nova IVF is one of India's largest fertility chains with over 80 centres nationally. The Andheri West branch is among Mumbai's most active, with a Google rating of 4.8 from over 300 reviews. Nova follows standardised protocols across all centres and is ICMR accredited. Pricing is chain-wide: base IVF from ₹1.5L, all-inclusive with meds typically ₹2L–₹2.5L. EMI available but not interest-free.

Indira IVF — Multiple Branches (Andheri, Thane, Navi Mumbai)

Indira IVF is India's highest-volume IVF chain with over 130 centres. Mumbai branches span Andheri, Thane and Navi Mumbai. Base IVF starts from ₹1.1L — among the most affordable in Mumbai — with 0% EMI available. Medications add ₹60K–₹1L, bringing total cost to ₹1.7L–₹2.6L per cycle. Success rates of 50–55% per cycle for women under 35.

Bloom IVF — Lilavati Hospital, Bandra West

Bloom IVF (part of the Lilavati Hospital ecosystem) is Mumbai's premium fertility option. Located in Bandra West at one of Mumbai's most respected private hospitals, Bloom is NABH certified and run by Dr. Hrishikesh Pai, a nationally recognised fertility specialist. Base IVF ₹1.5L–₹2L; full cycle with medications ₹2.1L–₹3.5L depending on protocol. Success rates 58–60% per cycle for suitable candidates. No 0% EMI — standard EMI available.

How to Choose the Right Mumbai Clinic

Location matters significantly in Mumbai — Andheri, Thane and Navi Mumbai options are 20–30% cheaper than premium south/central Mumbai clinics and equally capable for most patients. If you have a complex diagnosis (poor ovarian reserve, recurrent implantation failure, advanced age), a specialist clinic like Bloom IVF may justify the premium. For standard IVF or IUI, Indira IVF or Nova IVF offer excellent value.

Mumbai tip: Always ask which hospital your egg retrieval (OPU) will be performed in — some clinic chains use partner hospitals which may add facility charges not included in the quoted price.

What's Included vs Excluded in Mumbai IVF Prices

Typically included in the base price

  • Initial consultation with fertility specialist
  • Baseline investigations (ultrasound + hormone panel)
  • Egg retrieval (OPU) procedure and anaesthesia
  • Embryo culture in lab (3–5 days)
  • Embryo transfer procedure
  • Luteal phase support for 2 weeks post-transfer

Almost always billed separately (ask upfront)

  • Stimulation injections — ₹35,000–₹1,00,000 depending on protocol and response
  • ICSI — ₹15,000–₹30,000 add-on if needed for male factor
  • Embryo freezing — ₹15,000–₹35,000 for unused embryos
  • PGT genetic testing — ₹50,000–₹1,00,000 if required
  • Repeat monitoring scans beyond the package
