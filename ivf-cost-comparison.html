Why the USA costs 8–15× more

  • Healthcare system overhead and insurance billing
  • Liability and malpractice insurance costs
  • Higher physician salaries and facility costs

Indian IVF for international patients — what to know

🛫 Medical tourism for IVF in India

  • India is Asia's top IVF destination for international patients
  • Major clinics have dedicated international patient coordinators
  • Total cost including flights and hotel still far below home country IVF cost
  • English-speaking doctors, international-standard labs
  • Typical stay: 14–18 days for one complete fresh IVF cycle

📋 ART Act 2021 — India's regulatory framework

  • All IVF clinics must register with the National ART Registry
  • ICMR accreditation standards enforced
  • Mandatory quality standards for embryology labs
  • Foreign patients must comply with ART Act eligibility criteria
  • Commercial surrogacy prohibited for foreigners since 2021

🏥 Best cities for IVF in India

  • Mumbai — Largest number of accredited clinics. ₹1.7L–₹2.8L typical
  • Delhi NCR — Widest price range. ₹1.8L–₹3.0L typical
  • Bangalore — Most affordable. ₹1.6L–₹2.6L typical
  • Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune — growing IVF hubs, often 10–20% cheaper
