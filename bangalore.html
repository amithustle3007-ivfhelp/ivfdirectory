How to choose an IVF clinic

1
Get 2-3 quotes. Prices vary 3x. Always ask for all-inclusive written estimates.
2
Check the doctor. Confirm who actually performs your procedures - not just who you consult.
3
Ask live birth rate. Not pregnancy rate - for your age group specifically.
4
Budget for 2 cycles. Most patients need more than one. Plan finances upfront.
