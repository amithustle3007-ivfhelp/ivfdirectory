Guide to Bangalore's Top IVF Clinics

Milann Fertility — Kumara Park East, Bangalore

Milann (formerly BACC — Bangalore Assisted Conception Centre) was founded by Dr. Kamini Rao, one of India's most respected reproductive medicine pioneers. Established in 1990, Milann is among the oldest and most experienced fertility centres in India. Located in Kumara Park East, rated 4.7/5. Base IVF ₹1.25L–₹1.75L; with medications ₹1.75L–₹2.6L. EMI available through partner NBFCs. For patients wanting the most experienced hands in Bangalore, Milann is the top choice.

Nova IVF — Indiranagar / Jayanagar, Bangalore

Nova IVF's Bangalore centres cover Indiranagar and Jayanagar, serving east and south Bangalore. ICMR accredited with a 4.7 rating. Base IVF ₹1.4L–₹1.75L; with medications ₹1.9L–₹2.4L. Bangalore pricing is slightly lower than Nova's Mumbai and Delhi centres, reflecting the city's lower operating costs. EMI available but not interest-free. A reliable mid-range option with nationwide chain support.

Indira IVF — Koramangala / Jayanagar / Whitefield, Bangalore

Indira IVF's Bangalore branches span Koramangala, Jayanagar and Whitefield, covering south and east Bangalore effectively. Base IVF from ₹1.1L — Bangalore's most affordable major chain — with 0% EMI available. Medications add ₹40K–₹1L, total cost ₹1.5L–₹2.6L. Rated 4.3 with solid patient volumes and consistent protocols across all centres.

Why Bangalore IVF is Cheaper than Mumbai and Delhi

Bangalore's IVF costs run 5–10% below Mumbai and Delhi on average. The primary driver is lower real estate costs — clinic rental in Bangalore is significantly cheaper than Bandra or Gurugram. This saving is largely passed on to patients. Clinical quality at the top Bangalore centres is equivalent to any Indian city, and Milann's lineage makes it genuinely world-class.

Bangalore tip: Traffic is a real factor — an IVF cycle requires 5–8 visits over 2 weeks. Choose a clinic within 30 minutes of your home or office. Koramangala, Jayanagar and Indiranagar all have good options across budget tiers.

What's Included vs Excluded in Bangalore IVF Prices

Typically included in the base price

  • Initial consultation with fertility specialist
  • Baseline investigations (ultrasound + hormone panel)
  • Egg retrieval (OPU) procedure and anaesthesia
  • Embryo culture in lab (3–5 days)
  • Embryo transfer procedure
  • Luteal phase support for 2 weeks post-transfer

Almost always billed separately (ask upfront)

  • Stimulation injections — ₹35,000–₹1,00,000 depending on protocol and response
  • ICSI — ₹15,000–₹30,000 add-on if needed for male factor
  • Embryo freezing — ₹15,000–₹35,000 for unused embryos
  • PGT genetic testing — ₹50,000–₹1,00,000 if required
  • Repeat monitoring scans beyond the package
