Frequently Asked Questions — IVF in Bangalore

What is the cost of IVF in Bangalore in 2026?▼
IVF in Bangalore costs Rs1.1L-Rs3.1L per cycle all-inclusive - generally 5-10% cheaper than Mumbai and Delhi. Indira IVF starts from Rs1.1L. Milann Fertility charges Rs1.25L-Rs2.6L all-in. The most affordable metro for IVF in India.
Which is the best IVF clinic in Bangalore?▼
Top-rated Bangalore IVF clinics include Milann Fertility Kumara Park (Dr. Kamini Rao, Padma Shri), Mathrutva Koramangala (Dr. Manjunath C.S., 7,000+ IVF cycles, 70% success), and Nova IVF Indiranagar (Israel and Germany trained team).
Why is IVF cheaper in Bangalore than Mumbai and Delhi?▼
Bangalore has lower operating costs - real estate, staff salaries and overhead are lower than Mumbai and Delhi. This makes the same clinic tier approximately 5-10% cheaper. Bangalore also has strong competition among quality clinics which keeps prices competitive.
Does Indira IVF Bangalore offer 0% EMI?▼
Yes - Indira IVF offers 0% interest EMI across all Bangalore branches. Nova IVF and Cloudnine also offer EMI but may charge interest. Milann Fertility offers standard EMI. Always confirm the exact terms and processing fees.
