Guide to Delhi's Top IVF Clinics

Risaa IVF — Green Park, South Delhi

Risaa IVF is Delhi's highest-rated clinic by Google reviews (4.8 from over 620 reviews) and among the most affordable in the NCR. Located in Green Park, South Delhi. Base IVF from ₹1.2L with 0% EMI available. Full cycle with medications typically ₹1.7L–₹2.5L. Risaa reports a 65% success rate — a high number for the price point, making it exceptional value for patients in south Delhi.

Birla Fertility & IVF — Gurugram, MG Road

Birla Fertility is Delhi NCR's premium chain, backed by the CK Birla Group. The Gurugram centre on MG Road is NABH certified. Base IVF from ₹1,71,100 (published on their website) with full cycle including medications reaching ₹2.3L–₹4.2L. Birla offers 0% EMI and a dedicated patient coordinator. Success rates of 60% per cycle for women under 35. The right choice for patients wanting premium facilities and Gurugram convenience.

Nova IVF — Rajouri Garden, West Delhi

Nova IVF's Rajouri Garden branch serves west Delhi and is ICMR accredited with a 4.7 rating. Base IVF ₹1.5L–₹1.75L, total with medications ₹2L–₹2.5L. A reliable mid-range choice for west Delhi patients wanting chain-level protocols without Gurugram premium pricing.

Cloudnine Fertility — Punjabi Bagh, West Delhi

Cloudnine operates within the Cloudnine Hospital network (known nationally for maternity care). The Punjabi Bagh centre is NABH certified and rated 4.5. Base IVF ₹1.2L–₹1.8L; with medications ₹1.8L–₹2.8L. A solid choice for patients in west and north-west Delhi wanting hospital-level facilities at reasonable cost.

How to Choose the Right Delhi Clinic

Delhi has the widest price range of any Indian city. Gurugram (Birla, Fortis) commands a 30–40% premium over south Delhi (Risaa, Green Park). For standard IVF or IUI without complex diagnosis, Risaa IVF offers outstanding value. For advanced cases — recurrent failure, poor ovarian reserve, advanced age — Birla Fertility's specialist depth may justify the premium.

Delhi tip: Gurugram clinics are 20–30% more expensive than equivalent south Delhi options. If you live in south Delhi, Green Park or Vasant Kunj options are equally capable and significantly cheaper. Factor in travel — you'll make 5–8 visits during a cycle.

What's Included vs Excluded in Delhi IVF Prices

Typically included in the base price

  • Initial consultation with fertility specialist
  • Baseline investigations (ultrasound + hormone panel)
  • Egg retrieval (OPU) procedure and anaesthesia
  • Embryo culture in lab (3–5 days)
  • Embryo transfer procedure
  • Luteal phase support for 2 weeks post-transfer

Almost always billed separately (ask upfront)

  • Stimulation injections — ₹35,000–₹1,00,000 depending on protocol and response
  • ICSI — ₹15,000–₹30,000 add-on if needed for male factor
  • Embryo freezing — ₹15,000–₹35,000 for unused embryos
  • PGT genetic testing — ₹50,000–₹1,00,000 if required
  • Repeat monitoring scans beyond the package
