What should I ask at my first IVF consultation?▼
(1) What is the total all-inclusive cost including medications, ICSI if needed, and monitoring scans? (2) What is your clinic live birth rate for my age group — not pregnancy rate? (3) How many cycles do you recommend I budget for? (4) Will you personally perform my egg retrieval and embryo transfer, or a colleague? (5) What is your protocol for poor ovarian responders or previous failed cycles?
What is the consultation fee for an IVF specialist in India?▼
Consultation fees range from Rs500 to Rs3,000. Chain clinic consultations (Nova IVF, Indira IVF, Risaa IVF) are typically Rs500-Rs1,000. Senior specialists at private hospitals (Lilavati, Jaslok, Fortis, Birla Fertility) charge Rs1,500-Rs3,000. Many clinics offer a free initial phone consultation before the in-person visit — always ask.
