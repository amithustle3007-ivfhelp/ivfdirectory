About IVF Help

IVF Help is a free, independent resource dedicated to helping individuals and couples in India understand and compare the real costs of IVF, IUI, ICSI, frozen embryo transfer (FET), and egg freezing. We publish transparent, clinic-level pricing data across Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore, sourced from clinic websites, Practo, Vaidam, and verified aggregators, so that patients can make informed decisions without the stress of surprise fees or incomplete quotes.

Our goal is simple: to make fertility treatment costs clear, comparable, and accessible. We cover everything from what a base IVF cycle includes, to which add-ons are typically billed separately, to how success rates vary by age and clinic type — all in plain language, without requiring you to register or share your personal details.

Pricing data was last reviewed in April 2025. We update our comparisons regularly as clinic packages change. For the most accurate quote for your specific situation, we always recommend requesting an itemised estimate directly from your chosen clinic.
