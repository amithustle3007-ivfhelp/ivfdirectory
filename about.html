What We Do

IVFdirectory is an independent information platform dedicated to bringing price transparency to fertility treatment in India. We compile, structure and publish real pricing data from IVF clinics across Mumbai, Delhi and Bangalore so patients can compare costs before committing to treatment.

IVF in India is a ₹10,000+ crore market, yet most patients discover the true cost only after their first consultation — when medications, add-ons and hidden charges are revealed. We exist to change that. Our data covers IVF, IUI, ICSI, frozen embryo transfer, egg freezing, and donor egg IVF across all major clinic chains including Nova IVF, Indira IVF, Birla Fertility, Cloudnine, Bloom IVF, Milann, and Risaa IVF.

How Our Data Works

We compile pricing information from three sources, each assigned a confidence level:

High confidence — prices sourced directly from the clinic's own website or published price list. These are the most reliable.

Medium confidence — prices sourced from verified health aggregators such as Practo, Vaidam, and Justdial. Reliable but may lag behind clinic updates.

Low confidence — prices derived from patient forums, third-party blogs, or older sources. Shown as indicative estimates only.

We review and update our data regularly. All prices include the date last reviewed. If you believe any information is inaccurate, please contact us at the address below.
